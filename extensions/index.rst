Extensions
==========

Platformus consists of 4 extensions: Core, Website, ECommerce, and Images.
You can `write your own ones <https://platformus.readthedocs.io/en/latest/advanced/extensions_development.html>`_
to reuse code and to speed up development of the similar projects.
